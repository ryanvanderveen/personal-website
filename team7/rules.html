Rules:

Rock Climbing

  • Make sure your pockets are empty
  • Shoes tied
  • Harness is tight
  • Have an instructor check your harness
  • Make sure an instructor clips you in
  • Do not swing on the ropes
  • HAVE FUN!

Ropes Course

  • Helmet on and buckled
  • Shoes tied
  • No running
  • No jumping
  • No horseplay
  • Do not touch the overhead track system
  • Sling line should be in front of you the entire time
  • Harness is tight
  • instructor clips you in
  • ENJOY!

Zipline

  • One person on the line at a time
  • Listen to your leader
  • Keep hands on top of trolley at all times
  • Make sure your harness is tight
  • Buckle your helmet
  • Lift legs before touching down to the end platform
  • Don't panic if you don't make it to the end, we will pull you in
  • HAVE FUN!
