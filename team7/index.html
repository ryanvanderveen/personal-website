Providing the Allendale community with adventure since 2023.

Allendale Adventure Park is home to the best rope courses and ziplines in all of West Michigan! With multiple courses and activities, Allendale Adventure Park is the perfect place to test your limits and have fun as you adventure through the beautiful nature of Allendale.

With our rope and zipline courses, participants have the opportunity to climb and glide between trees in the Grand Ravines and along the Grand River! Participants can choose between many different adventures, with fun guaranteed!

Allendale Adventure Park Hours

Rope Courses: Everyday from 7am to 7pm

Ziplines: Everyday from 7am to 7pm

Rock Walls: Everyday from 7am to 7pm

Safety Note:

While adventure can be exhilarating, we believe the most fun comes when participants trust in the safety of our courses. We have advanced safety systems in place, with inspection approved and secured cables and harnesses for our zipline and rope courses. We also provide approved padding for those climbing our rock walls.
