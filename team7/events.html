College Afternoons

Stop by every Thursday at 1:00 for a discouted price for college students!

Adventurers that show us a valid student I.D. can get 40% off tickets for the afternoon, allowing access to our treetop courses, ziplines, and trails.

Under the Lights

We are open late on Saturday nights for adventures under the lights!

Come spend the evening with us for ascending the treetops with lanterns hanging from the branches and with luminescent harnesses. Not open to children under 7.

Birthday Parties

You can book birthday parties with us!

We offer adventures for large groups, along with a spot to celebrate with food in the forest! Contact us to book it!
